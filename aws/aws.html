Compute

Customer organizations run diverse workloads (instances, constainers, and serverless computing) on the AWS compute platform.

With AWS compute customers pay only for the instance or resource you need, for as long as you use it, without requiring long-term contracts or complex licensing.

With EC2 Spot Instances customers can save up to 90 percent over on-demand pricing for instances, or with Savings Plans they can save up to 72 percent across instances, containers, and serverless on a single bill.
